Coding

My medium term goal is to transition to a career in software development so I force myself to spend at least 2 hours a day coding (if it's not work related, I work on side projects or follow challenges and tutorials online). I did not have a formal education in computer science (I majored in Economics) so I enrolled in a coding bootcamp in 2017. It was particularly challenging because back then, I was managing a team of 45 and the bootcamp demanded 20 hours of in office class a week with an at least 10 hour a week commute. I learned HTML, CSS, PHP and Laravel but most importantly I gained a supportive community of developers and career transitioners.
